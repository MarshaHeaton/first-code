Words Matter

Semantics is simply the study of what words mean in different languages. Coding language is no different.

What do Semantic Elements Do?

Semantic elements give some sort of meaning to the content, making it easy for search engines to make sense of it and make it more accessible. It also helps other developers understand your intentions of the page and makes it easier to work on eachother’s code. And, of course, semantic elements act as an equalizer for all of the sites that are not coded in English.
